Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

What is the Vintage Boat Association?

The Vintage Boat Association is a community of antique and classic boat enthusiasts dedicated to the preservation, restoration, and celebration of historic watercraft.

Who can join the association?

Anyone with a passion for vintage boats! Whether you own a classic boat, are interested in restoration, or simply appreciate maritime history, you are welcome to join.

How do I become a member?

You can register for membership on our Membership Page. Choose the appropriate membership level and follow the instructions to sign up.



Membership & Benefits

What are the benefits of membership?

Members gain exclusive access to boat shows, networking events, restoration workshops, newsletters, and discounts on vintage boat-related products.

How much does membership cost?

We offer different membership levels, including Individual, Family, and Business memberships. Prices vary, so please check our Membership Page for details.

Can I cancel my membership?

Yes, you can cancel your membership anytime by contacting our support team. However, membership fees are non-refundable.



Boat Registration & Restoration

How do I register my vintage boat?

You can register your boat by visiting our Boat Registration Page and filling out the required details.

Does the association help with boat restoration?

Yes! We offer restoration guides, expert workshops, and connect you with professionals who specialize in classic boat restoration.

Are there grants or funding available for restoration?

We occasionally partner with organizations that provide funding for restoration projects. Check our news section for any ongoing grant programs.



Events & Boat Shows

What types of events does the Vintage Boat Association host?

We host antique boat shows, restoration workshops, networking events, and guided boat parades throughout the year.

How do I participate in an event?

You can register for upcoming events on our Event Registration Page. Each event has its own set of requirements and registration process.

Is there a fee for attending boat shows?

Some events are free, while others may require a registration fee. Check the event details on our website for more information.

Can I showcase my boat at an event?

Absolutely! Members can submit their boat details through our event registration portal for a chance to be featured in upcoming shows.



Support & Contact

How do I contact the association?

You can reach us through our Contact Page or email us at support@vintageba.com.

How can I stay updated on new events and news?

Subscribe to our newsletter, follow us on social media, and check our website regularly for updates.
