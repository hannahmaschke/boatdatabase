About the Vintage Boat Association

Our Mission

The Vintage Boat Association is dedicated to preserving and celebrating the legacy of antique and classic boats in Canada. Our goal is to connect boat enthusiasts, promote restoration efforts, and educate the public on maritime history. We strive to keep the tradition of wooden boat craftsmanship alive through events, restorations, and community initiatives.

Our History

Established in the 1980s, the association began as a small group of dedicated boaters passionate about restoring and showcasing historic wooden boats. Over the years, it has grown into a nationwide community with members across Canada, hosting some of the most prestigious vintage boat shows and parades.

Join Our Community

Becoming a member of the Vintage Boat Association means joining a passionate community of individuals who share a love for classic boats. Whether you own a vintage vessel, are interested in restoration, or simply appreciate maritime history, there is a place for you here.

As a member, you gain exclusive access to events, restoration guides, and a network of experienced boat enthusiasts. Together, we can keep the history of classic boating alive for future generations.
