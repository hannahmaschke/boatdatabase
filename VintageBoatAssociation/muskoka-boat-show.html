Muskoka Antique & Classic Boat Show

Event Details

Date: July 13, 2025

Location: Muskoka Wharf, Gravenhurst, Ontario

The Muskoka Antique & Classic Boat Show is one of the largest and most prestigious vintage boat shows in Canada. Every year, classic boat enthusiasts from across the country gather at Muskoka Wharf to celebrate the rich history of wooden boats and maritime craftsmanship.

This event features a stunning lineup of meticulously restored classic wooden boats, including rare 1920s mahogany runabouts, sleek mid-century cruisers, and elegant sailing vessels. Visitors can meet boat owners, learn about restoration techniques, and enjoy a weekend full of activities.
