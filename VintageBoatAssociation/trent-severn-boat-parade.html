Trent-Severn Antique Boat Parade

Event Details

Date: September 7, 2025

Location: Peterborough, Ontario

The Trent-Severn Antique Boat Parade is a breathtaking journey through Canada’s most scenic waterway, featuring a fleet of historic wooden boats cruising through locks and picturesque canals.

This event is more than just a parade — it’s a celebration of Canada’s maritime history. Enthusiasts can participate in the "Legends of the Waterway" storytelling sessions, where experienced sailors share tales of adventure.

Exclusive Activities: Visitors can hop on vintage vessels for a hands-on experience, watch live boat-building demonstrations, and enjoy an evening lantern-lit boat procession, a magical sight on the water.

Highlights

  • 🌊 Stunning boat parade along the Trent-Severn Waterway.
  • 🎤 Live commentary on the history of classic boats.
  • 📷 Exclusive photography sessions for participants.
  • 🏅 Commemorative awards and recognition for boat owners.
